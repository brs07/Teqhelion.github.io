Whatsapp PIP Mode

What is Picture-in-Picture mode and what's new in PiP 2.0

It is annoying that a video link opened in whatsapp directs to a new window in youtube. this may cause disturbance in the chat and reopening after the completion of video.

WhatsApp PIP mode enables you to open the video in the same window while having a chat with your friends or other people. However,there are limitations to this feature as you cannot swipe left or right as the video shutsdown.Also you cannot switch between chats or apps while the video is playing

So they are testing other feature where you can view the media recipients name before sending pitures and videos.Apparently the other modes as Dark mode,Vacation mode,Touch ID,and Face unlock seems to be coming...
