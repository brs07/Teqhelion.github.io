IOS 13 beta version

The below article shows how to install ios 13 on your Device.

Before starting check if your device is eligible for the update

List of devices

  • iPhone SE
  • iPhone 6s and newer
  • iPod touch 7th gen
  • iPad 5th and 6th gen
  • iPad Air 2 and 3
  • iPad mini 4 and 5
  • All iPad Pros

Step 1:

You'll need to sign up and enroll your device through Apple's public beta website. If you've previously tested Apple beta software before, you can just sign in.

Step 2:

After your device has been enrolled, open up Safari and go to beta.apple.com/profileand click the "Download profile" button.

Step 3:

After enrolling click on "Download Profile".Wait until it downloads!

Step 4:

After the download Goto Setings and click on Profile Downloaded and then install it.
